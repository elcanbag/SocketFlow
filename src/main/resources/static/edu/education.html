Why SocketFlow?

SocketFlow makes real-time device telemetry and analytics simple. Instead of building your own backend and dashboards from scratch, you get:

  • Zero-install Dashboard: Access your data from any browser—no client software required.
  • Universal API: Send data from any HTTP-capable device using Basic Auth.
  • Flexible Exports: Download PDF, CSV, Excel, or JSON, for all time or any date range.
  • Built-in Charts: Visualize trends instantly with Chart.js integration.
  • Secure by Default: End-to-end encryption and per-device tokens.

Use Cases

  • IoT prototyping—connect your Arduino, Raspberry Pi, or ESP32 in minutes.
  • Industrial monitoring—track sensors across multiple sites securely.
  • Data science experiments—streamline data collection for ML workflows.
  • Educational projects—teach real-time analytics without complex setup.
