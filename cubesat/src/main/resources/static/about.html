About SocketFlow

Welcome to SocketFlow, your go-to platform for real-time device telemetry and analytics. Designed with IoT innovators, industrial operators, and data enthusiasts in mind, SocketFlow delivers seamless integration, secure data handling, and powerful visualization—all through a clean, intuitive web interface.

From registering new devices to exporting comprehensive reports, SocketFlow streamlines your end-to-end workflow. Whether you’re monitoring temperature sensors in a greenhouse, tracking humidity levels in a warehouse, or collecting custom metrics from your prototype hardware, SocketFlow has you covered.

Our Mission

At SocketFlow, we believe data should be accessible, actionable, and above all, reliable. Our mission is to empower organizations of all sizes to harness the power of real-time telemetry without the traditional complexity and cost. We aim to provide a platform that’s both robust enough for enterprise deployments and flexible enough for hobbyists and researchers.

  • Accessibility: Zero-install web dashboard, mobile-friendly design.
  • Flexibility: Support for any device with an HTTP endpoint and Basic Auth.
  • Scalability: From single-sensor setups to fleets of thousands, SocketFlow scales with you.
  • Security: End-to-end encrypted communications and secure storage.

Key Features

Device Management

Register, organize, and authenticate your devices effortlessly. Each device receives a unique access token for secure communication.

Field Configuration

Create custom fields (e.g., temperature, humidity, pressure) with unit support. Rename or reconfigure fields on the fly.

Real-Time Data

Poll your devices every few seconds to fetch the latest telemetry. Visualize up-to-date readings in tables or charts.

Data Export

Download full datasets or filtered ranges in PDF, CSV, Excel, or JSON. Automated naming ensures clarity and traceability.
